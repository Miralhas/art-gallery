Your Gallery

When thinking about your gallery, answer these questions:

  • What is it called and why?
  • What are its beliefs and principles?
  • Who do you want to come and how will you encourage them to visit?
  • What kinds of artworks and objects does it collect and why?
  • What types of events or programs will you host to engage your audience?
  • What kind of atmosphere or emotions do you want to evoke in visitors to your gallery?
  • How will your gallery contribute to the broader art community or society as a whole?

Design your own art gallery

Your task is to design your own art gallery or museum. Imagine that you're the decision-maker, responsible for everything from its appearance to its purpose and audience.

If you're unsure where to begin, consider a museum or gallery you've visited. Reflect on how you felt upon entering, what you remember about the experience, and what impressions it left on you.

Think about the purpose of your gallery. Is it to showcase a specific type of art, to provide an educational experience, or to be a place for art appreciation and exploration? Your design should reflect this purpose and create an environment that encourages visitors to engage with the art in a meaningful way.

Also, consider the audience for your gallery. Who will be visiting? What are their interests and expectations? How can you cater to these needs and preferences?
